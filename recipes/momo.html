Making Momos!

Momos might seem like they're impossible to make, but with a few simple steps, you'll be on your way to momo mania!

Momo is a type of steamed dumpling that originated from Tibet and has become a delicacy in Tibet, Nepal, Bhutan, and the Indian communities of Darjeeling, Sikkim, and Kalimpong

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Maida
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  • For filling:1 cup Carrot
  1. Place ingredients in a bowl
  2. Mix ingredients in the bowl
  3. Cook ingreidents out of the bowl
  4. Enjoy!
